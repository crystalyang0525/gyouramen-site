About GYOU RAMEN

A Bowl of Passion, Rooted in Toronto

Born from a love of authentic Japanese cuisine, GYOU RAMEN brings the warmth of Japan's ramen culture to the heart of Toronto's Yonge Street.

Every broth is slow-simmered for hours. Every noodle is made to the perfect chew. Every topping is prepped fresh — because a great bowl deserves nothing less.

Our Promise in Every Bowl

Slow-Simmered Broths

Our broths are prepared fresh daily, simmered for hours to develop deep, complex flavors that you can taste in every spoonful.

Authentic Techniques

We honor traditional Japanese ramen-making methods — from tare seasoning to noodle hydration — while adding a local Canadian touch.
